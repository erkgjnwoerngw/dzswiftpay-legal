Frequently Asked Questions

👤
Account Creation
How do I create a DZ SwiftPay account?
Download the DZ SwiftPay app, tap "Create Account", and enter your email address and a secure password. You will receive a confirmation email — click the link to activate your account, then complete your profile.
What are the requirements to open an account?
You must be at least 18 years old and a resident of Algeria. You will need a valid email address, a phone number, and a government-issued photo ID for identity verification.
Can I have more than one account?
No. Each person may hold only one DZ SwiftPay account. Creating multiple accounts is prohibited under our Terms of Service and may result in all accounts being suspended.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter the email address associated with your account. You will receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you do not see it in your inbox.
🛡️
KYC Verification
Why do I need to verify my identity?
Identity verification (KYC) is required by Algerian financial regulations to prevent money laundering and fraud. Without completing KYC, your account will have lower transaction limits and restricted features.
What documents do I need for KYC?
You will need a clear photo of the front (and back, if applicable) of a valid government-issued ID — this can be a national identity card, passport, or driving licence. You will also need to take a live selfie photograph at the time of submission.
How long does verification take?
Most verifications are reviewed within 1–3 business days. You will receive an in-app notification and email once a decision has been made. If additional information is needed, we will contact you directly.
My KYC was rejected. What do I do?
Check the rejection reason shown in the app. Common issues include blurry photos, expired documents, or a mismatch between submitted information and your ID. Correct the issue and resubmit through the Verify Account section of the app.
Is my identity document stored securely?
Yes. Your identity documents are transmitted over encrypted connections and stored in a secure document vault. They are only accessed by authorised compliance personnel and are never shared with third parties except as required by law. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
↔️
Transfers
How do I send money to another user?
Tap the Transfer tab, select "Send Money", enter the recipient's registered email address and the amount, then confirm. Funds are credited to the recipient's account instantly.
Can I cancel a transfer after sending?
Transfers between DZ SwiftPay accounts are processed instantly and cannot be reversed once completed. Please double-check the recipient's email address and the amount before confirming any transfer.
What are the daily transfer limits?
Limits depend on your KYC verification tier. Unverified accounts have the lowest limits. Complete your KYC verification to unlock higher daily and monthly transfer limits. Current limits for each tier are displayed in the Transfer screen before you send.
Can I transfer to someone outside DZ SwiftPay?
Currently, transfers are available between DZ SwiftPay account holders. We are working on additional payment methods. Mobile top-ups are available for supported Algerian network operators.
💳
Failed Payments
My payment failed. What should I do?
Check that you have sufficient balance and that you have not reached a daily or monthly limit. If your balance and limits are fine, wait a few minutes and try again. If the issue persists, contact our support team at dzswiftpayapp@gmail.com.
I was charged but the recipient did not receive the money.
This is rare but can occur due to processing delays. Wait up to 30 minutes and ask the recipient to refresh their account. If the issue persists after an hour, contact us at dzswiftpayapp@gmail.com with the transaction reference number and we will investigate immediately.
A top-up failed but my mobile credit was not added.
Mobile top-ups can occasionally take a few minutes to be applied by the network operator. If the credit has not appeared after 15 minutes, contact us with the transaction details and we will investigate and arrange a refund or retry if needed.
How do I dispute an unauthorised transaction?
If you see a transaction you did not authorise, contact us immediately at dzswiftpayapp@gmail.com. Include the transaction date, amount, and reference. We will begin an investigation and may temporarily secure your account while we review. Please also change your password immediately.

Security
I think my account has been compromised. What should I do?
Act immediately: change your password using the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen, then enable your device PIN or Face ID in the Security section of your profile. Contact us at dzswiftpayapp@gmail.com with URGENT in the subject line so we can review your account activity and apply additional protections.
How do I set up a PIN or Face ID?
Go to Profile → Security → Set PIN. After creating a PIN, if your device supports Face ID or Touch ID and you have enrolled it in your device settings, you can enable biometric unlock from the same screen.
Will DZ SwiftPay ever ask for my PIN or password?
No — never. Our support team will never ask you to share your password, PIN, or one-time verification codes via email, phone, or chat. If anyone claiming to be from DZ SwiftPay asks for this information, it is a phishing attempt. Report it to dzswiftpayapp@gmail.com immediately.
How do I report a suspicious message or scam?
Forward the suspicious email or take a screenshot of the suspicious message and send it to dzswiftpayapp@gmail.com with a brief description of what happened. Do not click any links or provide any information to the sender before contacting us.
